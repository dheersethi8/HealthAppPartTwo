Overview

In a densely populated country like India, there is a disparity between the number of patients to the number of specialists.

Artificial Intelligence acts as a tool to dramatically improve the accessibility, reach and affordability of health care systems. By expediting the process of detecting symptoms and diseases, it helps increase the chances of survival.

Health Mate aims to aid physicians in the decision making process, fasten the detection of diseases, and make health care more consumer centric. One can diagnose the following health conditions :

  • Breast Cancer
  • Diabetes
  • Heart Conditions
  • Liver Conditions
  • Kidney Conditions

Use Cases

  • To reduce reliance on specialists.
  • To aid the clinical decision-making process.
  • To improve the accuracy of diagnosis.
  • To increase efficiency of medical care.
  • To better clinical outcomes.
